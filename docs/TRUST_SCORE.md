# Trust Score

The Trust Score is a 0–100 heuristic that summarizes how risky a Base token looks based on the live market and activity data Bankrize pulls. It is a quick read, not a guarantee of safety.

## Bands

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 70–100 | Good — healthy liquidity and activity, no major red flags |
| 35–69 | Medium risk — some warning signs, do your own research |
| 0–34 | High risk — multiple red flags, treat with strong caution |

## What feeds the score

The score is derived from the data returned for the token, including:

- Liquidity depth (thin liquidity lowers the score)
- 24h volume and recent transaction activity
- Buy vs sell flow balance
- Token age (very new tokens score lower)
- Risk indicators such as mint/freeze authority flags and suspicious activity patterns

Each score comes with short, human-readable reasons so you can see *why* a token landed where it did.

## Important

- The score is a heuristic, not financial advice.
- A high score does **not** mean a token is safe or that liquidity is locked.
- A low score does **not** automatically mean a scam — always do your own research.
- Bankrize never claims liquidity is locked or that a creator holds/sold unless the data confirms it.
