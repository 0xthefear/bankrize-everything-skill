# Contributing to Bankrize Everything

Thanks for your interest in improving Bankrize Everything. This is a read-only, Base-only token-analysis skill + app.

## Ways to contribute

- **Bug reports** — open an issue with steps to reproduce, the token/contract used, and what you expected vs. what happened.
- **Feature requests** — open an issue describing the idea and why it helps. Keep it within scope (read-only, Base-only).
- **Docs** — fixes and clarifications to `README.md`, `docs/DOCUMENTATION.md`, or `SKILL.md` are always welcome.

## Scope rules (please respect these)

- **Read-only only.** No swaps, buys, sells, transfers, approvals, claims, mints, deploys, bets, automations, or signatures.
- **Base only.** Do not add support for other chains.
- **Bankrize as the source.** Don't swap in a different token-analysis workflow or present non-Bankrize data as Bankrize data.
- Do not reintroduce removed sections: Base Discovery, Watchlist, Creator Holders, Bankrize Verdict, Smart Tags.

## Pull requests

1. Keep changes focused — one logical change per PR.
2. Update `CHANGELOG.md` under `[Unreleased]`.
3. If you change app behavior, keep `README.md`, `docs/DOCUMENTATION.md`, and `SKILL.md` consistent.
4. Describe what changed and why in the PR body.

## Style

- Match the existing tone: concise, factual, no hype.
- Never promise guaranteed safety or claim locked liquidity / creator holdings unless the data confirms it.
